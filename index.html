Study and classification of orbits in a pseudo-Newtonian model of a circular restricted three-body problem with Kerr-like primaries

The aim of the project is to explore chaos in strong gravity regions via the use of particle dynamics in pseudo-Newtonian and post-Newtonian potentials. A pseudo-Newtonian potential for Kerr-like primaries was constructed and an investigation of the dynamics of a test particle around this circular-restricted-three-body system was undertaken. The evolution of the zero velocity surfaces of the system and the stability of the orbits were studied using various methods of chaos detection. like the Poincaré map and SALI (Small Alignment Index).
